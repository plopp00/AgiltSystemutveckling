SnakeBreak

Menu = F10

how to:

A game where you play as a snake and try to eat as much apples as you can.The apple will spawn randomly and for every apple you eat another one will reappear. For every apple you eat you grow longer and longer.You lose if you either hit the wall or bite yourself.
